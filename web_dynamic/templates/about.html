Exprecn is an app that seeks to use the power of software engineering to simulate the process of amino acid production in the cell.

Exprecn represents a pioneering effort to bridge the gap between software engineering and the life sciences. By offering a simulated environment for amino acid production, it has the potential to revolutionize various industries and deepen our understanding of fundamental biological processes. As it continues to evolve and expand, Exprecn promises to be a catalyst for groundbreaking discoveries and advancements in the fields of biotechnology and synthetic biology.

The main goal of this app is to simplify the understanding of expression of genetic material in living cells and to servre as an handy tool to access expression data.
